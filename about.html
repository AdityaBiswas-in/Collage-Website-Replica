Who We Are

Bhawanipur Global Campus (BGC), formerly NSHM Knowledge Campus, Kolkata, is an initiative of the pioneer educational institution The Bhawanipur Gujarati Education Society (BGES). Together, BGES and NSHM Knowledge Campus Kolkata bring a combined legacy of more than 90 years, shaping generations of thinkers, innovators, and leaders.

BGC stands as a beacon of academic excellence, blending rich heritage with the dynamism of modern education. In a rapidly evolving world, it doesn't just adapt — it leads. The campus empowers students to dream, discover, and redefine possibilities, ensuring they graduate industry-ready, empathetic, and prepared to become independent thinkers and purposeful leaders.

The campus is situated at 80, B.L. Saha Road, Kolkata — 700053, and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Business, Allied Health, Pharmacy, Computing, Media, Design, and Hospitality.

Our Legacy

With roots going back over 90 years through The Bhawanipur Gujarati Education Society (BGES), BGC carries forward a proud tradition of academic and civic leadership in Kolkata.

BGES was founded with the vision to uplift the educational landscape of the region, and today BGC continues that mission with modern programmes, world-class infrastructure, and an unwavering commitment to student success.

1930s
Bhawanipur Gujarati Education Society established — laying the foundation of educational leadership in Kolkata.
2000s
NSHM Knowledge Campus, Kolkata founded — bringing professional and technical education to the forefront.
2020
BGES and NSHM join forces — combining 90+ years of legacy with modern academic excellence.
2023
Rebranded as Bhawanipur Global Campus — a bold step towards a future-focused identity.
2024
Awarded NAAC A+ Accreditation — recognition of academic excellence and institutional quality.
