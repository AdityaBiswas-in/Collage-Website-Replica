ADMISSION PROCESS

BGC offers a smooth and well-structured admission process. This systematic approach has helped students choose the right career path with confidence and clarity.

With over 65 courses and multidisciplinary programmes, the admission process is designed to ensure that students do not feel confused while selecting their field of study. Students are required to appear for an admission test and a personal interview, which they must clear to secure admission. Throughout the process, admission counsellors patiently interact with students to understand their interest-driven fields, strengths, and aspirations. This guidance helps students select a course that aligns with their passion, leading to better engagement and outcomes. The counselling sessions also provide a clear understanding of the programme structure, learning experience, and career opportunities, helping students form a complete picture before making a decision. At BGC, students can experience a hassle-free admission process. Our counsellors guide applicants at every step, ensuring they have all the information needed to move forward confidently. To complete the admission process online, follow these simple steps:

1

Fill up the application form: Submit your application easily by providing the necessary details for eligibility review.

2

Sit for the online aptitude test: Appear for the test as part of the evaluation process.

3

Interview with faculty members: Engage in a personalised interaction to clarify doubts and receive expert guidance on your chosen course.

4

Receive selection letter via email: Get confirmation of your selection along with further instructions for the next steps.

5

Pay admission fees: Secure your seat through a convenient online payment process and begin your academic journey smoothly.

Eligibility Criteria

BGC welcomes students from diverse academic backgrounds. The basic eligibility requirements for our programmes are:

  • ✓ Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognised board for UG programmes
  • ✓ Minimum 45% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • ✓ A relevant UG degree for PG programme admissions
  • ✓ Valid score in aptitude / entrance tests (where applicable)
  • ✓ No age bar — open to all eligible candidates
