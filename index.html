About Bhawanipur Global Campus

An autonomous NAAC A+ institution affiliated to MAKAUT

Bhawanipur Global Campus (BGC), formerly NSHM Knowledge Campus, Kolkata, is an initiative of the pioneer educational institution The Bhawanipur Gujarati Education Society (BGES). Together, BGES and NSHM Knowledge Campus Kolkata bring a combined legacy of more than 90 years, shaping generations of thinkers, innovators, and leaders.

BGC stands as a beacon of academic excellence, blending rich heritage with the dynamism of modern education. In a rapidly evolving world, it doesn't just adapt — it leads. The campus empowers students to dream, discover, and redefine possibilities, ensuring they graduate industry-ready, empathetic, and prepared to become independent thinkers and purposeful leaders.
